The web and internet along with it have tremendously improved since their inception.

Web technologies were extremely underdeveloped for the first couple of decades upon the introduction of the internet and web. There was no bullet-proof solution on how to handle and display the hundreds of different languages around the world on the web. This made it terribly difficult to communicate with others who used a different language other than English. Web security was also largely non-existent. Any information inputted into the web could easily be intercepted by a clever individual and used for harmful activities.

However, as of recent times this has all started to change. We are now introducing web security technology to better protect our private information. As a result, this has allowed online businesses to flourish. There has also been a formal solution which has solved the language barrier that prevented the web from truly being accepted by the masses. Now, the community is focusing on how to better protect our privacy on the web as it has become a hot topic as of late. Additionally, A.I's are becoming incredibly more complex allowing us to gather more information through these tools or accomplish more specific tasks with their help.
